# Privacy Policy for ForgeCV

**Last Updated:** February 4, 2026

ForgeCV ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use the ForgeCV Chrome Extension.

## 1. Data Collection and Usage

ForgeCV is designed with privacy in mind. We minimize data collection and prioritize local storage.

### A. Personal Information (Resume Data)
- **What:** We process the personal information contained in the resumes you upload (Name, Email, Phone, Experience, etc.).
- **How:** This data is parsed and stored **locally** in your browser's storage (`chrome.storage.local`).
- **Why:** To allow the extension to generate tailored resumes based on your profile.
- **Sharing:** This data is sent **only** to your selected AI provider (Google Gemini or Groq) for the sole purpose of generating your resume content. We do not store this data on our own servers.

### B. Website Content (Job Descriptions)
- **What:** We read the text content of the web page you are currently viewing when you click the "Process Page" button.
- **How:** This is done via the `activeTab` permission.
- **Why:** To analyze the job description and key requirements needed to tailor your resume.
- **Sharing:** This text is sent to your selected AI provider for analysis.

## 2. API Keys
- Any API keys you provide (e.g., Gemini API Key, Groq API Key) are stored **locally** in your browser.
- They are never sent to our servers.
- They are used strictly to authenticate requests with the respective AI providers.

## 3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to provide core functionality. Their use of your data is governed by their respective privacy policies:

- **Google Gemini API**: Used for AI text generation. [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Groq API**: Used for AI text generation. [Groq Privacy Policy](https://wow.groq.com/privacy-policy/)

## 4. Data Security
- Your data is stored locally on your device.
- Transmission to AI providers occurs over secure HTTPS connections.
- We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the Chrome Web Store support page.
